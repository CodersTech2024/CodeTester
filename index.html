Common Questions About Climate Change ü§î

  1. What is climate change?

    Climate change refers to long-term changes in global temperatures and other atmospheric conditions, often caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels.

  2. How does climate change affect the planet?

    It leads to more extreme weather, rising sea levels, melting ice caps, and threats to ecosystems and biodiversity.

  3. What causes climate change?

    The main cause is the increase in greenhouse gases from burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial activities.

  4. What are greenhouse gases?

    These gases, like carbon dioxide and methane, trap heat in the atmosphere, causing the Earth's temperature to rise.

  5. How can we stop climate change?

    We can reduce greenhouse gas emissions by using renewable energy, conserving energy, and supporting sustainable practices.
